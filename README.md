About the game:
This game takes place in a park where you meet a girl named Yuki, who seems to be irritated by the sun.
She will become interested in knowing you, and will ask for your help and what to do to get rid of the warmness of sun.
If the player chooses to help Yuki, later on in the game the player can choose how to help her and go on a journey with her.
If the player chooses the right paths, the player will be able to find out why Yuki hates the sun!

Software used to make the game: Renpy
Type of game: Visual novel

How to play:
1. Go to this link: https://anya369.itch.io/the-dim-light (This is the newer version of the game. The game has been updated and is not to date with this repo, it is up to date with the repo "the-dim-light2")
2. There should be a gray screen showing in the middle of the page. Click the button saying "RUN GAME".
3. Wait for the game to load
4. Now you are in the game screen!
5. a) to start the game, click start.
   b) to load past saved paths, click load
   c) To edit the game settings (audio, text speed, ect...), click preferences.
6. When starting the game, you will see Yuki talking.
   a) to make her continue talking, press enter/ click on the game screen.
   b) after at least one gameplay, you will be able to skip the dialogue. In order to do that, in the bottom click skip.
   c) to Save/Q.save, you can click the related buttons at the bottom of the screen!
   d) clicked something wrong in the gameplay? You can go back with clicking "back" at the bottom!
8. To choose an option in the gameplay, click the desired option.
9. To Load a saved scene, go to lthe main screen, click load, and choose your desired saved option!

End goal: Finish all 8 endings!
